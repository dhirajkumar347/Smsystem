How to Apply?

Contact the Admission Department

The concerned staff in the enquiry section will be very happy to help you in the Admission process

Make a Application

Applicants will be required to submit the following information to the Admissions Department to proceed with an application:
1.Application Form
2.Previous three years' school reports (including current year)
3.Copy of passport/Aadhar
4.Six passport size photographs

Sit for Entrance Assessment

Once your Application Form and Fee have been received, applicants will be required to sit an Entrance Assessment at the appropriate time. Successful applicants will be invited for an interview before a decision on their application is decided on.
